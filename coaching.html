Considering grad school—or leaving it behind?

Whether you’re preparing for graduate applications or stepping away from academia, I help you figure out what’s next with clarity, compassion, and a concrete plan.

  • Graduate school prep: Application strategy, statements of purpose, and interview practice
  • Career transition support: Resume and LinkedIn optimization, job search strategy, and mock interviews
